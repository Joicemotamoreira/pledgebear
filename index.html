How It Works

Setup takes just minutes, even if you have zero technical skills.

1. Pick Your Name

Choose a unique link for your page (pledgebear.com/YourName) that's easy for supporters to remember and reflects you or your cause.

2. Design Your Page

Customize the look with your image, logo, and message. Set suggested contribution amounts or let supporters choose their own.

3. Connect Payments

Link your existing Stripe account securely in seconds. All contributions flow directly to you.

4. Test & Launch

Preview your page to make sure everything looks perfect, then start sharing instantly!
