About Pledge Bear and Flip-Pay

PledgeBear is a simple and accessible way for communities, clubs, charities, and causes to collect contributions online. Whether you’re raising funds for your local football team, supporting a neighborhood project, or backing independent news, PledgeBear helps you accept donations without the hassle.

We're proudly developed by Flip-Pay, a platform that powers digital payments and monetization tools for media publishers and content creators. At Flip-Pay, we believe in giving everyone, not just big brands, the tools to grow sustainably through community support.

PledgeBear is part of that mission: no complex setup, no monthly fees, just an easy way to share your story and receive support from those who care.

Together, we’re building a better internet, one that empowers local voices, small groups, and real people.

PledgeBear helps creators and causes accept contributions from their supporters in a friendly, simple way.
