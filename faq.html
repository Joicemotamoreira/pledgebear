Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PledgeBear

What is PledgeBear?

PledgeBear is a simple, user-friendly platform that helps creators, non-profits, and small businesses accept online support from their community. We make it easy to create a beautiful contribution page in minutes, with no technical skills required.

Who can use PledgeBear?

PledgeBear is perfect for:

  • Content creators (artists, writers, musicians, podcasters)
  • Non-profits and charities
  • Small businesses and freelancers
  • Community organizations
  • Anyone looking to accept online support in a simple way

Is PledgeBear available in my country?

PledgeBear is currently available in the UK, US, and EEA (European Economic Area). We're working on expanding to more regions soon!

How do I create my PledgeBear page?

Creating your page is simple:

  1. Sign up for a free account
  2. Choose your unique page name (pledgebear.com/YourName)
  3. Customize your page with your image and message
  4. Connect your Stripe account
  5. Start sharing with your supporters!

Do I need a Stripe account?

Yes, you'll need a Stripe account to receive payments. If you don't have one, we'll guide you through the simple setup process. Stripe is our secure payment processor and ensures your supporters' payments are handled safely.

Can I customize my page?

Absolutely! You can customize your page with:

  • Your profile picture or logo
  • Custom colors and branding
  • Personal message to supporters
  • Suggested contribution amounts
  • Thank you messages

What are PledgeBear's fees?

PledgeBear charges a simple 4% platform fee per successful contribution. There are no monthly fees or setup costs. Standard Stripe payment processing fees also apply:

  • 2.9% + 30¢ (US)
  • 1.5% + €0.25 (EEA)
  • 2.5% + €0.25 (UK)

How do I receive my contributions?

All contributions go directly to your connected Stripe account. You can transfer funds to your bank account according to your Stripe payout schedule. Most users receive their funds within 2-7 business days.

What payment methods do you accept?

Through Stripe, we accept all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and more. We also support Apple Pay and Google Pay for mobile users.

What features does PledgeBear offer?

PledgeBear includes several helpful features:

  • Customizable contribution page
  • QR code generation for easy sharing
  • Embeddable widget for your website
  • Automatic thank you messages
  • Referral program for supporters
  • Analytics dashboard
  • Support for one-time and recurring contributions

How can I share my PledgeBear page?

Sharing your PledgeBear page is easy! You can copy and share your unique link, post it directly to social media, or generate a QR code to display on flyers, screens, or anywhere your supporters might see it.

Is PledgeBear mobile-friendly?

Yes! Your PledgeBear page is fully responsive and works great on any device. Supporters can view your page, scan your QR code, and make contributions seamlessly from their phones or tablets—no app needed.
